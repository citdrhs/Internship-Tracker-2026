Welcome to the CIT Internship Tracker.

A platform for students to log daily work, for mentors to provide actionable feedback, and for teachers to view progress over time.

What this website is for

Students

Submit a daily worklog with what you worked on, questions, reflections, and next steps.

Mentors

Submit feedback with ratings plus specific action items and focus areas to guide improvement.

Teachers

Review student progress and mentor feedback to support performance and accountability.

How it works

Log -> Review -> Improve

Worklogs capture daily progress; mentor feedback turns that progress into clear next steps.

Action Items

Feedback includes specific action items so students always know what to do next.

Focus Areas

Mentors can call out themes to focus on (e.g., stronger code comments, cleaner commits, testing).

Commonly Asked Questions(FAQ)

When should I submit a worklog?

Submit it after each day you work so your progress and questions are updated and frequent.(REQUIRED)

What makes good mentor feedback?

Concrete examples + specific action items (“do X by Friday”) + focus areas (“improve code commenting”).
